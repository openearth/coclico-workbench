    Explore the STAC Catalog

    CoCliCo SpatioTemporal Asset Catalog

    The CoCliCo platform offers structured access to datasets on coastal climate risks, sea level rise, extreme weather events, and adaptation strategies. These datasets are managed within a SpatioTemporal Asset Catalog (STAC), facilitating efficient search and retrieval of geospatial data.

    Dataset Categories

    The table below outlines key dataset categories available in the STAC platform.

    Category Description Data Layers
    Sea Level Rise Projections Regional and global sea level rise forecasts based on AR5 & AR6 climate models, including high-end scenarios. - AR5 Sea Level Rise
    - AR6 Sea Level Rise
    - Global Sea Level Projections
    Coastal Hazards & Flood Risk Storm surge levels, flood extents, expected damages, and total water levels under RCP4.5 & RCP8.5 scenarios. - Coastal Flood Risk
    - Coastal Hazard Flood Projections
    - Extreme Surge Level
    - Floodmaps
    - Flood Statistics by LAUs
    Coastal Exposure & Vulnerability Erosion risk classifications, asset mapping, infrastructure and population exposure, and coastal hazard projections. - Coastal Erosion Typology
    - Coastal European Exposure
    - Critical Infrastructure Spatial Index
    - Population Projections
    - Overture Building Footprints
    Shoreline & Coastal Morphodynamics Long-term shoreline evolution, coastal transects, and future shoreline change predictions. - Global Shoreline Morphodynamics
    - Shoreline Change
    - ShorelineMonitor
    - Global Coastal Transect Repository
    - Global Coastal Transect System
    Coastal Hydrodynamics & Wave Climate Storm surge projections, wave energy flux, and total water level estimates for coastal target points. - Storm Surge and Wave Climate
    - Total Water Level
    - Wave Energy Flux
    - Extreme Sea Level
    Adaptation Planning & Cost-Benefit Analysis Cost-benefit analyses of raising coastal defenses at the NUTS2 level under different climate and socio-economic scenarios. - Cost Benefit Coastal Adaptation
    Coastal Terrain & Geomorphology Global coastal digital terrain models (DeltaDTM) and shoreline morphodynamics. - DeltaDTM
    - Coastal Mask
    Administrative & Statistical Boundaries Local administrative units (LAUs), NUTS regions, and flood statistics aggregated by administrative boundaries. - Local Administrative Units (LAUs)
    - Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics (NUTS)
